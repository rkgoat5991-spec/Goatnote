About Goatnote

Empowering independent artists. No contracts. No fees. All vibes.

Our Story

Goatnote began as a passion project by a teenage artist who wanted to release music without industry nonsense. It quickly became a home for other independent artists looking for creative freedom and a supportive community.

100% Artist-Owned

You keep all your royalties—always.

No Contracts

Come and go as you please. No strings attached.

No Fees

Our mission is music, not profit.

How Goatnote Works

  • Apply: Submit your music with a simple form—no strings attached.
  • Release: We handle distribution to all major platforms via LabelLift.
  • Get Paid: You keep 100% of your royalties, always.

What Makes Us Different?

  • We’re run by artists, for artists.
  • No AI-generated music allowed—just real, human creativity.
  • Genre diversity welcomed. Lofi, rock, ambient frog synthcore—if it’s made with love, it belongs here.
  • Direct support: you’ll always get a reply from a real person (usually rkgoat!).
