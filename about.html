About Goatnote

Empowering independent artists. No contracts. No fees. All vibes.

Our Story

Goatnote is a small, independent music label and distributor started by a teenage artist who wanted to release music without all the industry nonsense. It began as a personal project but quickly turned into something bigger—a space for other independent artists who just want to make and share their music freely.

100% Artist-Owned

You keep all your royalties—always.

No Contracts

Come and go as you please. No strings attached.

No Fees

Our mission is music, not profit.

How Goatnote Works

  1. Apply: Submit your music with a simple form—no strings attached.
  2. Release: We handle distribution to all major platforms via LabelLift.
  3. Get Paid: You keep 100% of your royalties, always.

What Makes Us Different?

  • We’re run by artists, for artists.
  • No AI-generated music allowed—just real, human creativity.
  • Genre diversity welcomed. Lofi, rock, ambient frog synthcore—if it’s made with love, it belongs here.
  • Direct support: you’ll always get a reply from a real person (usually rkgoat!).
