Frequently Asked Questions

What is Goatnote?

Goatnote is a community-driven music label where independent artists can collaborate and distribute music without fees.

How do I join?

Simply head to the Sign Up page and fill out the form. We'll review your submission and get back to you!

Does it cost anything?

Nope! Goatnote is completely free. We’re here to support artists, not profit from them.

Where will my music be distributed?

We distribute to all major platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, and Deezer.

Can I leave anytime?

Yes, you’re free to leave Goatnote whenever you want. Your music rights stay with you always.
