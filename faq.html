Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Goatnote?
Any independent artist making original music (no AI-generated or copyright-infringing work) is welcome to apply.
How do I submit my music?
Just hit "Apply Now" at the top and fill out the Google Form with your details and links to your music.
Do you take any cut of my royalties?
Nope! You keep 100% of your royalties. Goatnote does not take a share.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No contracts, no obligations. You can leave or request to take your music down at any time.
How does distribution work?
We use LabelLift to send your music to all major streaming platforms. You’re always credited as the artist.
How long does it take to get my music live?
Usually a few days, but timing can vary depending on the streaming platforms.
What genres do you accept?
Any genre! Lofi, electronic, rock, ambient, experimental—if it’s made with love, it belongs here.
Can I use samples?
Only if you have the legal right to use them. No unlicensed or stolen samples.
What if I have more questions?
Contact us anytime. We reply fast!
