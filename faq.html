Frequently Asked Questions

What is Goatnote?

Goatnote is a non-profit music label dedicated to helping independent artists get their music heard without the stress of distribution costs.

Does it cost money to join?

Nope. Goatnote is 100% free — no hidden charges, no small print.

Where will my music be distributed?

Your music can go on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Amazon Music, and more.

How do I apply?

Just head over to the Apply Page and fill out the form!
