Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Goatnote for?

Goatnote is for independent artists who want a simple, hassle-free way to get their music onto streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and more, without giving up ownership or dealing with complex contracts. It's for artists who believe in community over corporate nonsense.

How do I join Goatnote?

You can apply to be a part of the Goatnote family by filling out our application form. We review every submission personally to make sure the vibe is right. If you're a good fit, we'll reach out to get started!

How do royalties work?

Through our distribution partner, LabelLift, you receive 90% of your royalties directly. The remaining 10% is a small royalty cut that helps us cover our basic operating costs and grow the label to support more artists. We are a non-profit label, and this cut is the bare minimum to keep us going.

Do you own my music if I release with you?

Absolutely not. You retain 100% of the rights to your music. We are simply a distribution channel to help you reach a wider audience. Your songs, your art, your ownership—always.

What genres do you accept?

All of them! We love music that is made with heart, no matter the genre. From lo-fi beats and hip-hop to rock, electronic, and experimental music, if you made it with love, we want to hear it.
