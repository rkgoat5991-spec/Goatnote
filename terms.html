Terms & Conditions

By joining Goatnote, you agree to the following:

  • You retain full ownership of your music. Goatnote does not take royalties.
  • Goatnote only distributes your music and helps you grow as an artist.
  • You are responsible for ensuring that your music does not infringe on copyrights.
  • Respect the community — we’re all here to support each other.

Goatnote is run with love and respect for independent musicians. By applying, you’re agreeing to vibe with that energy ✨.
